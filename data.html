We make our datasets publicly available for research and transparency. If you are interested in more specific information such as post level data, please fill out our Request Form . If you are requesting additional post level data, you will be required to sign our Data Sharing Agreement, and we are only allowed to share specific information as regulated by Meta "copywrite" in lieu of our contract.
